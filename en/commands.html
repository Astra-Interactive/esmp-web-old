Plugins of EMPIRE PROJEKT:

Standart asset of commands.
The server has an arena where things and experience remain after death / warp ARENA
/warps - view portals
/repair - repair the item in hand - after that also type /emrepair
/menu - open the server menu
Their prices can be viewed in / einfo prices

Smoke appears at the place of your death, which will be there until someone will pick it up (so things do not disappear after death).
/souls to see nearby places of death.

/menu.

Sorts things in your inventory and chests with the middle mouse button.[Donate access]

Allows you to make colored inscriptions on plates and rename items in colored text. Color information / einfo colors.[Donate access]

A shop where you can buy everything except potions and enchantments.
Command / shop / ds qsell or through the server menu.
The store is only available on the spawn, where it is located.

Allows you to sell your items on the general market.
Items will be there for 7 days, then they will disappear. You can remove them.
/ ah sell [price] - sell an item in hand
/ ah menu - open the market. Not available in end and nether.

Now you can sit on the steps and all that. You can sit / lie on the ground using /gsit /glay

You can create custom pictures on the server. You can open the link ImageMaps on site. [Pictures with obscenities and incomprehensible names will be removed!]
/imagemap <Название> download [URL]
/imagemap <Название> scale [with height]. [Example: /imagemap mapName scale 1 1]

You can create your own clans on the server.
/ clan modtag NewName - change the color in the clan. / einfo colors
/ clan and / clan help - help you[Only for Resident]
