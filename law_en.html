Guardians

There is a Guardian role on the server. Their job is fight against crime.
The guards have access to the command / position [player], which shows the player's location once every minute. The team is available on the territory of the prison.
Guardians can also look at the players' inventory / invsee [player] once per minute.
If the criminal killed or robbed someone, and there is no member of the Inquisition on the server, and the criminal refuses to pay a fine and (or) return things to the victim, then the guard has the right to kill the criminal and take his things.
With an online member of the Inquisition, a guard has no right to harm a criminal without permission inquisition, except for the attack of a criminal.

Inquisition

There is an Inquisition role on the server. Like the guardians, they fight crime, however, members of the Inquisition, unlike the guards, can punish sinners with impunity and put them in jail. / jail NAME Name Prison 10m
It is forbidden to plant a person for more than 1 hour.
The Inquisitor can land a player only if he is in his field of vision
The inquisitor must write in law-and-order how much and whom he imprisoned

Oracle
